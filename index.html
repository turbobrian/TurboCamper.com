Value

Add a safety equipment, food, water and off you go sailing. The Hobie 33 is known for having the highest bang per buck of nearly any boat. Turbo Camper epitomizes this with a proven design on an impeccably prepared boat.

Speed

Turbo Camper is fast and easily-driven. It completed the 2012 SHTP in 12 days 21 hours with no fuss. The boat is well prepared for offshore sailing. She surfs easily and doesn't load up in the transition above hull-speed the way wider boats do. Cruising above 10 knots is normal any time the spinnaker is flying.
